Welcome to the MapScore website. MapScore scores probability maps for Wilderness Search & Rescue (WiSAR) so the WiSAR community can compare methods for generating such maps. MapScore requests users to generate maps for actual historical searches (from ISRID), and keeps leaderboard statistics for each model or method. MapScore is part of the SARBayes project.
