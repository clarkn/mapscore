Grid Alignment Instructions


The first time you test a new model, you have to complete a grid alignment test, to make sure your model knows which way is up.

Given the (lat, lon) boundary points for the region, you must create a regular grid of 5001 x 5001 rectangular cells. We will then give you 9 (lat, lon) test points. You must return the corresponding grid coordinates:




Grid cell indexing for the maps behaves so that the upper left corner is (0,0). The upper right corner is (0,5000). The bottom left corner is (5000, 0). The bottom right corner is (5000, 5000). The LPS is located in the center cell of the grid, (2500,2500).

Note: We use a Great Sphere approximation of the Earth.
